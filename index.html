How to Use

Right Now! is a simple project reminder. It's not a to-do list or project manager. It's just a reminder. After downloading and installing Right Now! locally, set the index.html file as your home page, open a new tab and click on the "Wouldn't you rather be:" line.

The idea is simple: every time you open your browser or click "New Tab," you'll get the Right Now! page. With it is a reminder that time is fleeting, you have only so much time until you, too, will be a brain in a jar full of formaldehyde, and a list of what you consider you really would rather be doing right now. Clicking outside the edit area will return your list to normal.

All the data is stored locally using the browser's own local storage mechanism. There is no server, and no data is ever sent anywhere. Everything on your screen is yours, kept as secure as you keep your physical hardware.
